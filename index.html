Producers & DJs, want to play with us?

First off we're honored you'd think about joining us. Whether you've been pluggin away in the bedroom or playing out for years, we welcome you all.

That said we're shooting for a certain sound with a certain set of skills to go with it. This is the bar we've set for ourselves and we're asking people that play with us to reach for it too. We're going to be asking everyone to audition (either live or via a live recording) and these are the things we're looking for.

Underground Dance Music

We're lovers of all kinds of music but Flavorless is about pushing forward a particular sound. Namely underground house and techno. Think Nicolas Jaar & Acid Pauli.

Seriously Tight Mixes

We want to hear those mixes locked in. Attention to track phrasing a must. Beatmatching should be nearly flawless or brought quickly back in when out of sync.

Harmonic Mixing

Tracks should sing together. You're likely using some software, Mixed in Key is our fav, to find your track keys and then mixing them based on the camelot system.

Professionalism

Showing up on time. Not being a mess during your set by drinking too much. Ending when you're supposed to.

Creativity

Surprise us with your track selection or creative use of samples.

Attention to Detail

All of the above and more will add up to a serious attention to detail.

All the above sound like you? Get in touch with us below!
